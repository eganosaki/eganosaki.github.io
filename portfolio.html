Website Restructure

San Diego Public Library

Identifying a problem with the current site layout for the San Diego Public Library, I developed an alternative.

I began by carrying out a content inventory of the current site.

Then I carried out testing to determine better ways to organize the content of the site.

Finally, I restructured the site and developed a solution for two particular workflows within a mobile app.
